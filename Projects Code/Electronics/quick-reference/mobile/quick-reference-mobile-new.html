Essential electronics formulas & standards

Ohm's Law

V = I × R
• V = Voltage (volts)
• I = Current (amperes)
• R = Resistance (ohms)
I = V / R
Calculate current from voltage and resistance
R = V / I
Calculate resistance from voltage and current

Power Calculations

P = V × I
Power (watts) = Voltage × Current
P = I² × R
Power from current and resistance
P = V² / R
Power from voltage and resistance

Resistor Combinations

Series: Rtotal = R₁ + R₂ + R₃...
Resistors in series add up
Parallel: 1/Rtotal = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + 1/R₃...
For two resistors: Rtotal = (R₁ × R₂) / (R₁ + R₂)

Capacitor Formulas

XC = 1 / (2πfC)
Capacitive reactance (ohms) at frequency f
Series: 1/Ctotal = 1/C₁ + 1/C₂...
Capacitors in series (like resistors in parallel)
Parallel: Ctotal = C₁ + C₂ + C₃...
Capacitors in parallel add up
