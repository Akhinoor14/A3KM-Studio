KUET Debating Society (KDS)

KUET Debating Society (KDS) is the student's organization of debating society in Khulna University of Engineering and Technology. KDS has been working for the popularization of debate in the South-west region of our country for almost a decade. The aim of KDS is to institutionalize the practice of logic and intellect, increase consciousness about various national and international issues, thus creating a responsible, competent future generation for the welfare of our nation. KDS has been awarded twice by BDF (Bangladesh Debating Foundation) as best regional debating club.
