SECTION 01

Paper Listing & Search

The paper listing (paper-listing.html) presents papers as styled cards with title, authors, abstract snippet, journal/conference name, year, and topic tags. Full-text search and sidebar filters narrow results instantly.

SECTION 02

Paper Viewer & Abstract

The paper viewer (paper-viewer.html) embeds the PDF directly for reading. The left panel shows title, abstract, keywords, authors, and publication info. The right panel renders the PDF via embed. DOI link opens the original source.

SECTION 03

Citation Export

Each paper detail page provides one-click citation export in three formats: APA, IEEE, and BibTeX. Clicking a format button copies the formatted citation to the clipboard. Useful for academic referencing.

প্রতিটি paper-এ APA, IEEE এবং BibTeX format-এ citation export করা যায়। Button click করলে clipboard-এ copy হয়।

APA Format
Islam, A. K. (2024). Title. Journal, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/…
BibTeX Format
@article{key, author={…}, title={…}, journal={…}, year={2024}} — ready for LaTeX
